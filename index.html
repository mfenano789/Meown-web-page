Do Cats know their name?

Cats can learn to recognize and respond to their names, but the extent to which they do so can vary greatly from cat to cat. Some cats readily respond to their names and show recognition by turning their heads, twitching their ears, or even approaching their owners when called. However, other cats may not respond as consistently or at all.
